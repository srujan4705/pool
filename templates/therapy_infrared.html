The Science Behind Infrared

Far infrared waves penetrate deep into your body's tissues, causing a rise in core temperature and promoting cellular regeneration. This process, known as hyperthermia, enhances natural detoxification pathways.

Unlike traditional saunas, infrared technology heats your body directly rather than heating the air around you, allowing for longer, more comfortable sessions with enhanced therapeutic benefits.

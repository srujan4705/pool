The Science Behind EMS

EMS Training uses electrical impulses to stimulate multiple muscle groups simultaneously. These impulses mimic the natural action of your central nervous system, causing muscles to contract.

The technology allows for deeper and more intense muscle contractions than conventional exercise, leading to more efficient workouts and faster results.

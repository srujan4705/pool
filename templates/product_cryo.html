About LifeCube Cryotherapy

The LifeCube Cryotherapy Chamber represents the pinnacle of German engineering in wellness technology. This state-of-the-art whole-body cryotherapy solution delivers controlled exposure to extremely cold temperatures, ranging from -80°C to -110°C, in a safe and nitrogen-free environment.

Unlike traditional cryotherapy chambers that rely on liquid nitrogen, LifeCube utilizes an advanced electric cooling system, making it both environmentally friendly and significantly safer for operators and clients. The chamber's sophisticated temperature control system ensures precise and consistent treatment conditions, while the spacious interior accommodates comfortable treatment sessions.

02

The Science Behind Oxygen Therapy

Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy works by dramatically increasing the amount of oxygen your blood can carry. Under normal conditions, oxygen is primarily transported by red blood cells. During HBOT, oxygen dissolves directly into all body fluids.

This oxygen-rich environment reaches damaged tissues that normal oxygen levels cannot penetrate, triggering cellular repair, reducing inflammation, and activating the body's own healing mechanisms. The increased pressure also reduces bubble size in the bloodstream, helping to treat conditions like decompression sickness.

What to Expect

Your hyperbaric oxygen therapy session will be comfortable and relaxing:

  1. You'll change into comfortable clothing provided by our center
  2. Enter our state-of-the-art hyperbaric chamber and lie down
  3. The chamber will gradually pressurize while you breathe normally
  4. Relax, read, or meditate during your 60-90 minute session
  5. The chamber will slowly depressurize at the end of your session

Most clients report feeling refreshed, energized, and clear-headed after their session.

The Science Behind the Chill

Whole Body Cryotherapy works by triggering the body's natural healing mechanisms. When exposed to extreme cold, blood vessels constrict, reducing blood flow to the extremities and redirecting it to vital organs.

Once you exit the chamber, oxygenated blood rushes back to peripheral tissues, delivering nutrients and removing toxins. This process, known as vasoconstriction followed by vasodilation, is what creates the therapeutic effects.
